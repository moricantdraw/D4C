Design for Church

Designing meaningful change, together.

A modular, collaborative program where church leaders tackle real challenges in their congregation using a hands-on, co-design approach — and grow a supportive network of peers along the way.

Apply Now

Our Mission

Churches face new challenges every day — from growing pains to engagement slumps to digital transformation. Design for Church exists to equip church leaders with the tools, confidence, and community they need to respond creatively and collaboratively. Through a structured design process, leaders will not only solve a current challenge in their congregation but also build lasting skills for tackling future problems with their communities, not just for them.

Who Is it for?
  • Pastors and ministers
  • Church staff members
  • Ministry leaders or directors
  • Anyone with a leadership role and a problem to solve

You don’t need to be an expert. You just need to be willing to listen, try new things, and walk alongside others

What You'll Gain
  • A solution to a pressing problem in your church
  • Confidence in applying co-design and research techniques
  • A network of like-minded church leaders
  • Practice leading with humility, creativity, and collaboration
Community & Support
  • Small peer groups to walk the journey with you
  • Slack workspace for informal updates and feedback
  • Facilitators with experience in both church leadership and design
  • Guest speakers from church, nonprofit, and design worlds

FAQs

Do I have to be a pastor to apply?

Nope. If you have a leadership role in your church and a challenge you care about, you're welcome.

What kinds of challenges are appropriate?

Anything real and meaningful — examples include declining youth participation, volunteer burnout, communication gaps, or new ministry launches.

How much time will this take each month?

About 4–6 hours total per month, including virtual sessions and personal work time.

What if my project takes longer than 8 months?

That’s fine! Teams can work at their own pace. We'll continue offering support as needed

 Is there a cost to participate?

Currently, D4C is free thanks to generous donations. You just need access to a computer and internet connection
